Composition — AI Writing Tools

Think harder.
Write better.

These tools help you use AI as a thinking partner — not a ghostwriter. Fill in the form, copy the prompt, and paste it into Claude or ChatGPT. The AI will ask you questions and give you feedback. You do the writing.

Course AI policy: AI may help you think, question, outline, and get feedback. It may show you an example (like a sample thesis) to illustrate a point. It may not write your paragraphs, your sentences, or your ideas for you. Work you submit must be your own.
